How it works

Built to stay out of your way.

No window, no shortcuts to memorize. Klavi watches for the telltale gibberish of a wrong layout and quietly puts it right.

Automatic correction

The moment you finish a word, Klavi checks every enabled layout and fixes it if it only makes sense in another language.

Double-tap Shift

Convert or undo the last word by hand. Klavi learns your choice and won’t touch that word again.

Per-app memory

Klavi remembers the layout each app last used and restores it the moment you switch back.

Private by design

No analytics, no account, no network connection of any kind. Password fields are never read.

Privacy

Nothing leaves your Mac.

Klavi has no servers to talk to. Keystrokes are processed in memory, in real time, and never written to disk or sent anywhere.

  • No analytics, no tracking, no servers
  • No account — it works the moment you launch
  • Secure password fields are never read
